Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Stael Gissoni ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website staelfogarty.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when booking a session or contacting us:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Service preferences and booking details
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store your card details)
  • Any notes or messages you provide through the booking form or chatbot

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our site:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral sources
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see below)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your bookings
  • To communicate with you about your sessions
  • To send booking confirmations and reminders
  • To improve our website and services
  • To run targeted advertising on Facebook and Instagram
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior

Cookies & Tracking

Our site uses cookies and similar technologies from:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Facebook Pixel — to measure ad effectiveness and show relevant ads to people who have visited our site.
  • Stripe — for secure payment processing.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the site.

Payment Security

All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. We never see, store, or have access to your full credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Google — for analytics (anonymized)
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — for advertising optimization

Data Retention

We retain your booking and contact information for as long as needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Opt out of targeted advertising (via your Facebook ad settings)

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
